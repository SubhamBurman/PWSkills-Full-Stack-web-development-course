What is Physics Wallah

Physics Wallah (PW) is an Indian online educational technology company that provides affordable learning resources for students in grades 6 to 12.
PW was founded in 2020 by Alakh Pandey and Prateek Maheshwari.
Pandey started the company as a YouTube channel in 2014.
PW's headquarters are in Noida, Uttar Pradesh.
PW provides: Mock tests, Crash courses, Video lectures, Study materials, Practice tests.
