Question 2 : What are html entities? List out five commonly used html entities.

Solution : The reserved characters used in HTML Document are known as HTML Entities. 
They are not seen in typical keyboard. They provide a wide range of characters, 
allowing you to add icons, geometric shapes, mathematical operators, and so on.
Five commonly used html entities are:
1  &lt; - Represents the less-than symbol <.
2  &gt; - Represents the greater-than symbol >.
3  &amp; - Represents the ampersand symbol &.
4  &quot; - Represents a double quotation mark "
5  &copy; - Represents the copyright symbol Â©.
