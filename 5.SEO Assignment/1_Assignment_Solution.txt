Question 1 : what is SEO and explain the importance of SEO ?

Solution : SEO stands for Search Engine Optiimization. It helps you  optimize your 
websites so that they have better rankings on Search Engines like Google, Bing and Yahoo.
Importance -
The ultimate goal of SEO is to rank higher in search engine results pages for relevant 
keywords and phrases, which can increase visibility, traffic, and ultimately revenue for 
the website.
